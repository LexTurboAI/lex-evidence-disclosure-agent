# AI Act Article 50 — transparency context

This project references Article 50 of the EU AI Act as a transparency-oriented compliance context.

The MVP does not provide legal advice. It demonstrates a practical audit workflow for checking whether synthetic AI-agent logs contain basic disclosure, operator, scope, approval and audit-trail information.

For public-facing materials, avoid precise fine amounts unless verified against the final legal text and official guidance. Safer wording: **non-compliance with AI Act transparency duties may lead to significant administrative fines under Article 99.**
